# Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
---

**Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** adds an extra verification step at login on top of your password. After entering your password, you also provide a code from an authenticator app (such as Authy, Google Authenticator, or Microsoft Authenticator) or a biometric factor. This significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access, even if your password is compromised.

:::note
All steps below assume you are signed in to your <BrandName /> account. If you don't have an account, <a href="https://www.testmuai.com/register/" onClick={CookieTrackingSignup}>register for free</a>.
:::

## Enable User MFA
---

**Step 1:** From the **Profile** menu, select **Account Settings**.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/account-settings.webp').default} alt="Account Settings option in the Profile menu" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 2:** Open the **Passwords & Security** tab and click **Setup** next to **Authenticator App**.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/password-tab.webp').default} alt="Passwords & Security tab with Authenticator App setup option" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 3:** Scan the generated **QR code** with your preferred authenticator app (such as Authy, Google Authenticator, or Microsoft Authenticator), and enter the code shown in the app.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/user-auth.webp').default} alt="QR code and verification field for the authenticator app" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 4:** Copy or download the **recovery code**, select **I have written down this security code**, and click **Submit**.

:::warning
You will need the recovery code if you lose access to your authenticator app or delete it by mistake. Save it somewhere secure.
:::

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/user-recovery.webp').default} alt="Recovery code with confirmation checkbox" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

You have successfully enabled User MFA on your <BrandName /> account.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/user-setup-complete.webp').default} alt="User MFA setup complete confirmation" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>


## Disable User MFA
---

**Step 1:** From the **Profile** menu, select **Account Settings**.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/account-settings.webp').default} alt="Account Settings option in the Profile menu" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 2:** Open the **Passwords & Security** tab and click **Disable** next to **Authenticator App**.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/disable-user.webp').default} alt="Disable button next to Authenticator App" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 3:** Enter your account password to verify your identity.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/disable-one.webp').default} alt="Password verification step for disabling MFA" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 4:** Enter the **six-digit authentication code** generated by your authenticator app.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/disable-two.webp').default} alt="Six-digit authentication code field" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

MFA is now disabled on your account, and you will be redirected to the login page.


## Enforce <BrandName /> MFA for Your Organization
---

**Step 1:** Navigate to **Settings** and select **Organization Settings** from the dropdown.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/org-settings.webp').default} alt="Organization Settings option in the Settings menu" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 2:** Open the **Authentication and SSO** tab and **toggle on** the **Require MFA** option.

:::note
Users who have not set up MFA will be logged out immediately and asked to set up MFA the next time they sign in.
:::

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/auth-sso.webp').default} alt="Authentication and SSO tab with Require MFA toggle" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 3:** Log in again to set up MFA.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/setup.webp').default} alt="Login page after MFA enforcement" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 4:** Click **Set Up MFA**.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/setup-mfa.webp').default} alt="Set Up MFA button" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 5:** Scan the generated **QR code** with your preferred authenticator app, enter the verification code shown in the app, and click **Submit**.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/verification-code.webp').default} alt="QR code and verification field for organizational MFA" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 6:** Copy or download the **recovery code**, select **I have written down this security code**, and click **Submit**.

:::warning
You will need the recovery code if you lose access to your authenticator app or delete it by mistake. Save it somewhere secure.
:::

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/recovery-code.webp').default} alt="Recovery code with confirmation checkbox" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

You have successfully set up MFA for your <BrandName /> account.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/setup-complete.webp').default} alt="MFA setup complete confirmation" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 7:** Sign in again. Enter the newly generated **authentication code** from your authenticator app and click **Verify**.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/auth-code.webp').default} alt="Authentication code entry on login" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>


## Disable Enforced MFA for Your Organization
---

**Step 1:** Navigate to **Settings** and select **Organization Settings** from the dropdown.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/org-settings.webp').default} alt="Organization Settings option in the Settings menu" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 2:** Open the **Authentication and SSO** tab and **toggle off** the **Require MFA** option.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/toggle-off.webp').default} alt="Require MFA toggled off in Authentication and SSO" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>


## Disable <BrandName /> MFA for a User
---

**Step 1:** Navigate to **Settings** and select **Organization Settings** from the dropdown.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/org-settings.webp').default} alt="Organization Settings option in the Settings menu" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 2:** Open the **Members** tab.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/organization-settings.webp').default} alt="Members tab in Organization Settings" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

**Step 3:** Click the **three-dot (⋮) menu** next to the user's name and select **Disable MFA**.

<img loading="lazy" src={require('../assets/images/lambdatest-mfa/disable-mfa.webp').default} alt="Disable MFA option in the user's actions menu" width="404" height="206"  className="doc_img img_center"/><br/>

You have successfully disabled MFA for the user.
