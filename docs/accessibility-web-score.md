# Accessibility Score

Accessibility Score is a health metric that helps teams understand the overall shape of automated findings.

The Accessibility Score summarizes automated findings as a quick health signal you can compare across runs or releases. It helps spot regressions and improvement trends, but it is not equivalent to WCAG sign-off or legal compliance on its own. Always read the score alongside detailed issues and any manual verification your program requires before stakeholders draw conclusions.

## What the score is good for

- comparing runs over time
- spotting regressions or improvement trends
- getting a quick summary view before opening issue details

## Important note

The score is not the same as legal or standards certification. It should always be interpreted together with detailed findings, manual verification, and remediation context.
