## What is Real Device Testing?

<BrandName /> Real Device Testing lets you manually test your mobile apps and websites on real Android and iOS devices in the cloud, in a live interactive session — no physical device lab required. You get a real device streamed to your browser, with full access to gestures, hardware features, network controls, and debugging tools.

Use it to reproduce device-specific bugs, validate UI on real hardware, test features that emulators can't fake (camera, biometrics, SIM, GPS), and run accessibility checks with native screen readers.

## App testing vs. browser testing

Real Device Testing covers two distinct journeys. Pick the one that matches what you're testing:

| You're testing… | Use | Start here |
|---|---|---|
| A native or hybrid **mobile app** (`.apk` / `.ipa`) | **App Testing** | [Run Your First App Test](/docs/app-testing-on-real-devices) |
| A **website** in a mobile browser on a real device | **Browser Testing** | [Run Your First Browser Test](/docs/browser-testing-on-real-devices) |

Both journeys share the same in-session capabilities — simulate real-world conditions, test accessibility, and review results — applied to either your app or a browser.

## What you can do in a session

- **Manage and install apps** — upload builds, manage versions, or install directly from the Play Store, App Store, TestFlight, App Center, or Firebase. *(App Testing)*
- **Use in-session tools** — screenshots, recordings, file upload/download, ADB shell, and bug marking.
- **Simulate real-world conditions** — GPS geolocation, network profiles, SIM, camera image injection, biometric auth, dark mode, date/time, audio injection, and more.
- **Test accessibility** — navigate with TalkBack (Android) and VoiceOver (iOS) screen readers and adjust iOS accessibility settings.
- **Inspect and debug** — the UI inspector, on-device DevTools for WebViews, and live app performance profiling.
- **Review results** — replay sessions, view logs, and analyze artifacts in the [manual tests dashboard](/docs/manual-tests-dashboard).

<Note>
Some capabilities (App Distribution, Apple Pay, audio injection, app profiling, device passcode, screen readers) are **beta** or require enablement by your organization admin or <BrandName /> support. Each feature page notes its requirements.
</Note>
